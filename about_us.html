WHY choose Cambodia?

They need you and you need them.

The Australian Industry Trade College creates the best apprentices. We have a wealth of technical talent and we are aiming to use that talent to make a difference to the lives of underprivileged people of Cambodia. ​ Our values of courage and mateship are not just words.

Be brave , be generous and give back to improve the lot of these people.  ​

Critical poverty dictates their destiny

Education can change that.

 

When you help by providing supplies and support for the schools, more children can access education. We will build school classrooms, donate resources, teach skills and give hope.

 

Children who get an education can escape the limitations of poverty by gaining employment and provide for their families in the future.
