COST

Each participant will pay $3000 to cover all the costs of the trip. Money is raised through individual and goup fundraising projects. Money is collected through the Go Fundraise website.

We will be runnng a variety of fundraising activities to help team members to reach their target.

The money will cover flights, accommodation, transport, sight seeing and also contribute to funding the community projects.

ALL THE SITES

We will also have some time to enjoy the sites and unique culture of Cambodia. The itinerary will include visitis to: Angkor Wat, Floating Village, Night Markets and Rice fields

TRAVEL DETAILS

We will be comfortable in a local Siem Riep Hotel. Students and staff will share a room with one other person and have access to the hotel facilities including laundry, pool and games room.

 

Transport

We will use the local form of transport in and around Siem Riep. Tuk- tuks will deliver us to projects and move our group to all activities and sights

 

Flights

Flying with Jet Star we travel via Singapore to SIEM RIEP

 

DEPART BRISBANE:

Thursday 14th September

RETURN SIEM RIEP:

Wednesday 27th September

 

Comprehensive travel insurance is included, covering luggage, medical costs and accidents.

Travel documents

You will need a current passport and an entry visa for Cambodia will be arranged prior to departure
