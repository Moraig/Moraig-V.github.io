WHO is involved?

It takes a village to raise a child

STUDENTS

We will take about 30 of the AITC's finest students to Siem Reap to help in a variety of projects. You could be one of the select few who get to join us on the trip of a life time.

STAFF

We will take about 7 staff to supervise and help manage all the projects, including OHS officer, first aid trained staff and teaching staff. Our CEO and 2 members of the board, including our Chairman will join us.

PARTNERS

We are priveledged to have a variety of industry partners willing to join us and offer their expertise. We have several employers who will bring the trade experience and knowledge. We will benefit from the expertise of builders, carpenters, plumbers and landscapers.

NEW HOPE

They are a non government organisation based in Siem Reap who will be our hosts for the trip, offering connections and support for all the projects we will participate in. New Hope will coordinate the distribution of our donations and ensure that we are helping the most needy in the community

PARENTS

We are also lucky to have places for parents of AITC students who are willing to join us on the trip. They will enjoy the priveledge of working with the team and they will see and be a part of the good work you will be doing for the people of Cambodia.
